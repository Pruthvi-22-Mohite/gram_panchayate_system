Government Schemes & Subsidies

Find information about various central and state government schemes you may be eligible for.

Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) - Gramin

Provides financial assistance to the rural poor for constructing their own houses.

Key Eligibility:
  • Must not own a pucca house in any part of India.
  • Meets the socio-economic criteria as per SECC 2011 data.

Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA)

Guarantees 100 days of wage-employment in a financial year to a rural household whose adult members volunteer to do unskilled manual work.

Key Eligibility:
  • Must be a citizen of India and above the age of 18.
  • Must be part of a rural household and willing to do unskilled manual work.
Request Job Card
National Social Assistance Programme (NSAP) - Pension Schemes

Provides financial assistance to the elderly, widows, and persons with disabilities in the form of social pensions.

Key Eligibility:
  • Varies by scheme (e.g., age criteria for old age pension, disability certificate for disability pension).
  • Applicant must belong to a Below Poverty Line (BPL) household.
