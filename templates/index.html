Welcome to the Gram Panchayat Portal

Our digital platform is designed to empower our village community. We provide a single point of access for essential public services, community engagement, and transparent governance.

Explore this website to find emergency contacts, submit feedback and suggestions, track the progress of local projects, and access important public records. Our goal is to make village administration more accessible and efficient for every resident.





How to Access Gram Panchayat Portal

Follow these simple steps to log in and start using the services:

  1. Step 1: Go to the Login Page

    Click on the "Login" button at the top right corner of the home page or navigate directly to Login Page.

  2. Step 2: Enter Your Credentials

    Enter your registered Aadhaar Number and the One-Time Password (OTP) sent to your linked mobile number.

  3. Step 3: Verify Your Identity

    Upon entering the OTP, the system will verify your identity. Make sure your mobile number is updated with your Aadhaar details.

  4. Step 4: Access Your Dashboard

    Once verified, you will be directed to your personalized dashboard where you can access all available services and information.
