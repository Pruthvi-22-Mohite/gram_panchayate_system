Government Schemes & Subsidies

Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY)

Affordable housing for all by 2022. Provides financial assistance to EWS and LIG categories for construction of houses.

  • Financial assistance up to ₹2.5 lakh
  • Interest subsidy on home loans
  • Applicable for EWS/LIG categories

Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA)

Guarantees 100 days of wage employment in a financial year to rural households.

  • 100 days of guaranteed employment
  • Wage payment within 15 days
  • Unemployment allowance if work not provided
Check Eligibility Apply Now
Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY)

Provides LPG connections to women from BPL households to protect their health.

  • Free LPG connection
  • Deposit of ₹1,600 for LPG connection
  • Eligible for BPL households
Check Eligibility Apply Now
National Social Assistance Programme (NSAP)

Provides financial assistance to elderly, widows and disabled persons living below poverty line.

  • Old Age Pension: ₹200-1000/month
  • Widow Pension: ₹300-1000/month
  • Disability Pension: ₹300-1000/month
Check Eligibility Apply Now
Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY)

Financial inclusion programme aimed at providing universal access to banking facilities.

  • Zero balance bank account
  • Accidental insurance cover of ₹2 lakh
  • Overdraft facility after 6 months
Check Eligibility Apply Now
Ayushman Bharat - Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY)

Health insurance scheme for poor and vulnerable population with cover of ₹5 lakh per family.

  • Cover of ₹5 lakh per family
  • Pre-existing diseases covered
  • No cap on family size or age
